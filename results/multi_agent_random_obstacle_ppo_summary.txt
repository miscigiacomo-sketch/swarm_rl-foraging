Interpretation:
The centralized PPO policy is evaluated in a randomized-obstacle multi-agent foraging environment. The random baseline measures uninformed exploration, while the greedy obstacle-aware baseline uses local one-step distance minimization without full path planning. The deterministic PPO result measures argmax action selection, whereas the stochastic PPO result measures performance when sampling from the learned policy distribution.
